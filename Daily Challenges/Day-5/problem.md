- Given the array input. Print the pattern.

> Example 1 :

```
INPUT 1 : [7,9,11,5,4,2,8]

OUTPUT  :

         *
         *
     *   *
     *   *               *
 *   *   *               *
 *   *   *               *
 *   *   *   *           *
 *   *   *   *   *       *
 *   *   *   *   *       *
 *   *   *   *   *   *   *
 *   *   *   *   *   *   *
[7  9   11   5   4   2   8]
```

> Example 2 :

```
INPUT 1 : [4,8,2,9,0,4,2]

OUTPUT  :

             *
     *       *
     *       *
     *       *
     *       *
 *   *       *       *
 *   *       *       *
 *   *   *   *       *   *
 *   *   *   *       *   *
[4   8   2   9   0   4   2]
```

> Explanation: Print numbers of starts according to the value at particular index.
